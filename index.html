01  ·  The Beginning

It starts with
the yarn

In 1995, a unit in Ludhiana began selecting only the finest Giza cotton — combed, carded, chosen for hand-feel. Thirty years on, that same obsession with raw material quality is still our foundation. We are a Government Recognised Star Export House and Sedex-ETI certified because ethical trade isn't optional — it's how we do business.
